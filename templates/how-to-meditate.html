1) Take a seat

Find a place to sit that feels calm and quiet to you. After that, set a time limit

2) Feel your breath

Follow the sensation of your breath as it goes in and as ir goes out.

3) Notice when your mind has wandered

When you get around to noticing that your mind has wandered—in for a moment - simply return your attention to the breath.

4) Close with kindness

When you’re ready, open your eyes. Notice any sounds in the environment. Notice how your body feels right now. Notice your thoughts and emotions.
